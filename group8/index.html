About Us

Mission

At the heart of the Olympics 2024 is the celebration of unity, diversity, and sportsmanship. The Games aim to bring together nations from around the world, fostering a spirit of peace and excellence. As athletes push their limits, break records, and create unforgettable moments, the Olympics continues to inspire new generations.

New Sports and Sustainability

The 2024 Olympics will also feature new sports such as surfing, skateboarding, and sport climbing, offering fresh excitement for spectators. Paris is committed to making this the most sustainable Olympics ever, focusing on reducing carbon emissions and promoting eco-friendly solutions across all aspects of the Games.

Why Paris 2024?

Paris has a rich history with the Olympics, from the grandeur of 1900 when it hosted the second-ever modern Olympic Games to the spectacle of 1924. Now, a century later, Paris is ready to deliver yet another unforgettable chapter in Olympic history. With iconic venues such as Stade de France, and breathtaking new ones along the River Seine, the city promises a truly unique Olympic experience.

Team

The Paris 2024 organizing committee is composed of passionate individuals committed to delivering a seamless, unforgettable event for athletes, fans, and the global community. With experts from logistics, event management, sports, and sustainability, the team is working hard to make sure every aspect of the Games reflects the Olympic spirit of excellence and unity.
